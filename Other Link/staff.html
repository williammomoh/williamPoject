Deo Gahiza (Information System Specialist)

Background

Deo Gahiza has established himself as an entrepreneur in both the Information Technology and the Real Estate sector. In the IT sector, He has over 6 years of experience working as an IT consultant for Acrodex Inc. providing Advance Desktop and Networking services to various government ministries in Alberta, Canada, as well as designing and implementing security, firewall, encryption & VOIP solutions for corporate clients across Canada.

On the real estate front, for over 5 years Mr. Gahiza has owned a very successful real estate business Gahiza’s Realty & Investment Inc. His company have position itself strategically in the local market place. Deo is also a licensed Realtor in Canada and have won three of Re/Max prestigious clubs award (President, 100% & Platinum clubs). He is also a member of Re/Max International elite Hall of Fame Club.

Mr. Gahiza is a visiting instructor that volunteers with the College when he is in Liberia.

Qualifications

Deo Holds a Bachelor degree in Information System Technology, Masters in Information Systems Security Management and is currently completing his MBA in Risk Management.
