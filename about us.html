About Us

Bishop Grimley Hight School

Bishop Grimley Hight School

The Bishop Gremly High School is an institution that seeks better foundation for our Liberian children by giving them the best of learning and perfect environment as well. There are some main objectives of the Bishop Gremly High School and they are as follow:

• To promote sound education – promoting sound education is one of our main goal and we always looks forward to making our institution the number one foundation builder across the country.

• To provide good and moral conduct – with our train and qualify staff, we are ready to help every child possess a good character and a perfect moral conduct especially when outside the school walls.

International Testing Center

One of the biggest benefits we offer our students is the international testing center. The test center has been opened since October 1, 2016. The center provides our students and businesses the opportunity to write and become professionally certified right here in Monrovia. Their certification will be internationally recognized. With that certification, they will have the ability to work anywhere in the world including Liberia and the United States.

Our Training Offers

Each Subject is geared towards a specific knowledge base. The best and most unique thing about us is our teaching and delivery methods. We strongly believe in practical application and giving our students the hands on experience they need.
