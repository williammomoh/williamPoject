THE BISHOP GRIMLEY HIGHT SCHOOL – SERVICE TO GOD AND MANKING

 

The Bishop Gremly High Schoo was established in Liberia, March 22, 1987. .

The Bishop Gremly High School is an institution that seeks better foundation for our Liberian children by giving them the best of learning and perfect environment as well.

There are some main objectives of the Bishop Gremly High School and they are as follow:
• To promote sound education – promoting sound education is one of our main goal and we always looks forward to making our institution the number one foundation builder across the country.

• To provide good and moral conduct – with our train and qualify staff, we are ready to help every child possess a good character and a perfect moral conduct especially when outside the school walls.

Our training programs are as follows:
1. Diploma

Students have the opportunity to complete full studay and earn a diploma.

2. Certificate in Computer

The Certificate program is opened to everyone who desires to acquire specialized skills in Computer related areas and eam a Certificate
No entrance and placement exam is required for admission.

3.Sport

Enroll into our institution and get 100% Affordable and professional Education; Good English , Good Math, , Good History and E- Books. Candidates of these programs must complete an Entrance and Placement Examination for enrollment. Please refer to eligibility criteria section for entry into our School.
